# Ai engineer Course

**Data Analyst**
A Data Analyst collects, cleans, and analyzes data to find useful insights and present them through reports and visualizations. Their work helps organizations make informed decisions.

**Data Science**
Data Science is the field of using data, statistics, and programming to discover patterns, solve problems, and make predictions that support better decision-making.

**Business Analysts** 
A Business Analyst studies business processes and requirements, identifies problems, and recommends solutions that help organizations improve their performance and achieve their goals.

**Machine Learning (ML)**
Machine Learning is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed for every task.

**Generative AI (GenAI)**
Generative AI is a type of Artificial Intelligence that can create new content such as text, images, videos, music, and code by learning patterns from existing data.

**Agent AI**
Agent AI refers to intelligent systems that can understand goals, make decisions, use tools, and perform tasks autonomously with minimal human intervention.

***MultiAi Agent ***
A multi-agent agentic system is a collection of AI agents that can independently perceive information, make decisions, take actions, communicate with one another, and coordinate their efforts to solve complex tasks.
